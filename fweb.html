Introduction: Jute is the main cash crop of Bangladesh. Jute is Golden in color. It brings us foreign money. So, it is called “the golden fiber of Bangladesh”. Once upon a time, the economic development of Bangladesh was mostly dependent on jute and goods made of jute, but the present condition is not the same. Jute has lost its position. Description of Plant: Jute is a kind of fiber and it is collected from a plant. The plant grows from a height of six to ten feet. Generally, the jute tree has no branches. it has got barks and leaves which are bodily used.
